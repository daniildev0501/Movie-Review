Movie Review: Stranger Things

Season 1 of Stranger Things follows a group of kids in a small town after their friend Will disappears. A mysterious girl with powers, Eleven, helps them search for him. They discover a secret lab, a parallel dimension called the Upside Down, and a monster behind the disappearance. In the end, Will is rescued, but the danger is not fully gone.
